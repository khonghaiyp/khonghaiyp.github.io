About me ...!

I'm lecturer and researcher in Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics (FaMI), Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST)

I defended my PhD theis in 2023 under the joint supervision of Profesor Philippe Carmona and Director de recherche Sylvain Gandon . The title of my dissertation was "Epidemiology and evolution of infectious diseases in a periodic environment"
